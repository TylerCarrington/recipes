Double Chocolate Caramel Marshmallow Brownies

Dessert

Difficulty : Easy Source : Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups walnuts

1/2 cup agave

1 dash salt

2 tablespoons soy milk

2 cups spelt flour

1/4 cup cocoa powder

3/4 cup raw sugar

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon vanilla extract

1/2 cup Earth Balance® Organic Coconut Spread, melted

1/2 cup soy milk

1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

1 cup vegan mini-marshmallows (or large ones, quartered)

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Prepare the vegan caramel by processing the first 4 ingredients (walnuts through soy milk) in a food processor or high-speed blender.

Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl (spelt flour through salt). Add the vanilla, coconut spread and soy milk and stir until just combined.

Stir in the chocolate chips.

Place half of the batter into an oiled 9x9 square baking dish. Spread the caramel mixture over the entire bottom layer, then top with the remaining chocolate batter.

Sprinkle the marshmallows evenly over the top.

Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out mostly clean, then set on a cooling rack to cool before cutting.
