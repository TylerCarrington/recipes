Creepy Cookie Cake

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep Time: 20 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 Source: Riehle Family Secret Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 oz) pkg vegan cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup vegan sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3/4 container vegan cool whip
  • 2 cups crushed Oreos, filling removed
  • 1 (9 inch) Oreo pie crust
  • Or
  • 20 oreos crushed, filling removed
  • 4 tbsp vegan butter melted

Directions

Crust

If making pie crust from scratch, combine butter and 20 crushed oreos. Place mixture in pie pan and press flat to form crust

Filling

Combine softened cream cheese and sugar in mixing bowl. Beat on low speed until creamy.

Add sour cream and vanilla, beating until well combined.

Set aside 2 tbsp crushed for decoration.

Stir in remaining oreos and cool whip gently until just combined. Spoon mixture into pie crust.

Refrigerate for 4 hours to allow the pie to setup.

Sprinkle the reserved crushed oreos over the cake and serve.
