Easy Meatballs

Dinner

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 40 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: Servings 4 Source: Bettycrocker.com

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean (at least 80%) ground beef
  • 1/2 cup Italian-style bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup applesauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (1/4 cup)

Directions

Heat oven to 400Â°F. Line 13x9-inch pan with foil; spray with cooking spray.

In large bowl, mix all ingredients. Shape mixture into 20 to 24 (1 1/2-inch) meatballs. Place 1 inch apart in pan.

Bake uncovered 18 to 22 minutes or until no longer pink in center.
