Plant-Made Mini Pot Pies

Dinner

Difficulty : Easy Source : Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

2 cups flour, plus extra for sprinkling

⅔ cup Earth Balance® Soy Free Buttery Spread

¼ cup cold water

Dash of salt

1 tablespoon Earth Balance® Soy Free Buttery Spread

1 medium onion, diced

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup potato, diced

2 cups chopped vegan grilled chick'n strips, such as Beyond Meat or Gardein

2 cups vegetable broth

3 cups fresh or frozen mixed veggies

2 tablespoons tamari

¼ cup cornstarch

2 tablespoons nutritional yeast

Salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons Earth Balance® Soy Free Buttery Spread, melted to brush on top of crust

Directions

To prepare crust, place butter, flour, water and salt in the bowl of a food processor and pulse until thoroughly combined. Scoop out dough, pat into a ball, cover with plastic wrap and chill for 20 minutes.

Preheat oven to 400° and lightly butter the inside of 4 ramekins.

In a large pan set over medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon Earth Balance® Soy Free Buttery Spread. Add onion, garlic, potato and vegan chick'n strips and saute for 10 minutes, until potatoes are tender. Increase heat to medium-high and add all remaining ingredients. Let simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and set aside.

To prepare the crust, roll out dough on a lightly floured surface to a 1/3-inch thickness.

Cut circles on dough large enough to fit inside the ramekin, and press into the bottom of each ramekin. Cut the remaining dough into 1/4-inch strips.

Spoon vegetable mixture into ramekins until slightly over-filled. Use the strips of dough to create lattice-top. Brush the tops with melted Earth Balance® Soy Free Buttery Spread and bake for 25 minutes, until tops are golden.
