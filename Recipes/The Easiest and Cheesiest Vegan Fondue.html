The Easiest and Cheesiest Vegan Fondue

Dinner

Prep Time : 6 hrs Cook Time : 5 mins Difficulty : Easy Servings : Servings: 10 servings Source : Veganosity.com

Ingredients

2 cups raw cashews soaked overnight See Note

½ cup vegan cream cheese

1 cooked potato peeled

1 garlic clove

1 cup water

1/3 cup nutritional yeast

1 tbsp white wine vinegar

2 tbsp white miso

1 tbsp lemon juice

Sea salt to taste

Paprika to garnish optional


DIPPING OPTIONS

Cubed bread

Apple slices

Raw vegetables cauliflower, broccoli, bell pepper, carrots….

Pretzels

Directions

Soak the cashews in boiling water for at least 6 hours.

Drain and rinse the cashews and put in a high-powered blender with the remaining ingredients and blend on a high speed for approximately 2 minutes, or until it’s smooth and creamy. You shouldn’t detect any solid pieces. If you taste solid nuts then keep blending.

Notes

This recipe takes less than 5 minutes to make, the long prep time is because you need to soak the cashews, so don't let the time in the recipe card scare you.

For the creamiest fondue we recommend soaking the cashews for at least six hours or overnight.
