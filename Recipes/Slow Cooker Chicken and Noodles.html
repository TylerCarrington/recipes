Slow Cooker Chicken and Noodles

Dinner, Lunch, Slow Cooker

Prep Time: 30 m Cook Time: 8 h Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces bow tie (farfelle) noodles

Directions

Place chicken, broth, onion, celery, carrots, and salt and pepper to taste into a slow cooker. Add celery if desired. Set temperature to low and cook for 6 to 8 hours.

When chicken is tender, tear or chop into bite-sized pieces.

Cook noodles then add to soup for 15 minutes. Adjust seasonings to taste.
