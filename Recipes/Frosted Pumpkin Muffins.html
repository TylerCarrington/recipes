Frosted Pumpkin Muffins

Dessert

Difficulty: Easy Source: Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

  • MUFFIN
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup unflavored almond milk
  • 1/4 cup Earth Balance® Original Buttery Spread
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • FROSTING
  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in water 4-8 hours
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • Up to 1/4 cup unflavored almond milk

Directions

Muffin Directions Preheat oven to 350° F, and line 12 muffin cups with liners.

In a large mixing bowl, stir together flour, coconut sugar, baking powder, baking soda, spices and salt.

In a small bowl, stir together pumpkin puree, almond milk, Earth Balance® Original Buttery Spread and vanilla.

Add wet ingredients to dry and stir until just combined.

Divide mixture among muffin cups and bake 16-18 minutes or until tops spring up when pressed lightly.

Allow to cool completely before frosting

Frosting Directions Place cashews in food processor or blender and blend to a thick paste.

Add lemon juice and vanilla, blend.

Add powdered sugar slowly and blend.

Slowly add milk until desired thickness is reached.
