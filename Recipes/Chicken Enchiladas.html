Chicken Enchiladas

Dinner

Prep Time : 20 Cook Time : 45 Difficulty : Medium Source : Riehle Family

Ingredients

Filling:

2.5 cups cooked chicken

0.5 cup sour cream

1.5 tsp taco seasoning

1/2 can cream of mushroom soup

1 medium onion, chopped

1/2 cup salsa

1/4 cup sliced olives


Topping:

1 cup sour cream

1.5 tsp taco seasoning

1/2 can cream of mushroom soup


Other:

10 flour tortillas

0.5 cup cheddar cheese

Directions

Make filling by combining chicken, sour cream, taco seasoning, cream of mushroom, onion, salsa, and olives in a large bowl.

Place 1/3 cup filling in each tortilla. Roll up tortilla making sure the sides are folder over.

Place in 9 by 13 inch pan.

Combine sour cream, taco seasoning, and mushroom soup in a small bowl to make the topping. Spoon over tortillas.

Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Uncover and put cheese on top. Cook in oven until cheese is melted.
