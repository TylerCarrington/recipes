Chocolate No Bake Cookies

Dessert

Difficulty: Easy Source: Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups coconut sugar
  • ½ cup dairy-free milk
  • 1 Earth Balance® Vegan Buttery Stick, cut into large pieces
  • 3 tablespoons raw cocoa powder
  • ½ cup Earth Balance® Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats

Directions

To a medium saucepan set over medium-high heat, add sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa powder. Bring to a rolling boil and boil for 1 minute.

Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter, vanilla, salt and oats.

Drop large scoops of no-bake cookies onto parchment paper. Allow to dry for 10-15 minutes, until the no-bake cookies have become firm.
