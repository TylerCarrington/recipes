Eggless Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe (Vegan)

Breakfast

Prep Time : 15 minutes Cook Time : 20 minutes Servings : Servings 12 Source : Spiceupthecurry.com

Ingredients

1 cup almond Milk

1 teaspoon White distilled vinegar

2 cups All purpose flour

½ cup White granulated sugar

1 tablespoon Baking powder

¼ teaspoon Salt

½ cup Oil

1 teaspoon Pure vanilla extract

2/3 cups Chocolate chips

For topping:

½ teaspoon Ground cinnamon

1 tablespoon White granulated sugar

Directions

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C for at least 10 minutes. place the oven rack in center of the oven.

Line the 12 cups muffin pan with paper liners or just spray it with oil.

Making eggless chocolate chip muffins:

Add white vinegar to the milk. Keep it aside for 10 minutes. Meantime prep other ingredients for muffin

Mix the granulated sugar and cinnamon powder for topping and keep it aside till needed.

Now take all purpose flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a bowl. Mix it very well using wire whisk.

Make a well in center of the dry flour mixture. Add prepared milk mixture, oil and vanilla extract.

Beat it till it gets moistened. Do not over mix it.

Add chocolate chips. Fold it in.

Divide the batter in 12 muffin cups.

Sprinkle the topping mixture on top of batter.

Bake in pre-heated oven for 19-20 minutes or till the toothpick inserted in center of the muffins comes out clean. Then remove it on wire rack.

After 5 minutes remove the muffins from the pan and let it cool completely on wire rack.
