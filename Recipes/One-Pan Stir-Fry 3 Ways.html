One-Pan Stir-Fry 3 Ways

Dinner

Prep Time : 25 min Cook Time : 15 min Difficulty : Easy Servings : Servings: 2 Source : M.youtube.com

Ingredients

Stir fry sauce:

½ cup low sodium soy sauce

¼ cup honey

2 cloves garlic

1 teaspoon ginger

1 tablespoon sesame seeds


Beef and Broccoli:

1 pound flank steak, cubed

1 teaspoon pepper

2 cups broccoli, diced

1 portion stir-fry sauce


Pork and Asparagus Stir-Fry:

1 pound pork loin, cubed

1 teaspoon pepper

1 cup asparagus, trimmed and cut

1 cup red pepper, diced

1 portion stir-fry sauce


Chicken stir fry:

1 pound chicken, cubed

1 teaspoon pepper

1 cup broccoli, chopped

1 cup bell pepper, diced

1 portion stir-fry sauce

Directions

Mix together all ingredients in a small bowl.

Use sauce with your stir-fry of choice!

Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry:

1. Heat oil over a nonstick pan and add beef stirring until brown.

2. Pour sauce in pan and stir to coat meat.

3. Once the sauce is bubbling, add the veggies to the pan and stir again to coat.

4. Cook until meat is cooked through and veggies are soft.

5. Serve over rice or alone.

6. Enjoy!

Pork and Asparagus Stir-Fry:

Heat oil over a nonstick pan and add pork stirring until cooked.

Pour sauce in pan and stir to coat meat.

Once the sauce is bubbling, add the veggies to the pan and stir again to coat.

Cook until meat is cooked through and veggies are soft.

Serve over rice or alone.

Enjoy!

Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry:

Heat oil over a nonstick pan and add chicken stirring until cooked.

Pour sauce in pan and stir to coat meat.

Once the sauce is bubbling, add the veggies to the pan and stir again to coat.

Cook until meat is cooked through and veggies are soft.

Serve over rice or alone.

Enjoy!
