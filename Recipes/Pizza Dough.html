Pizza Dough

Dinner

Prep Time : 15 m Cook Time : 15 m Difficulty : Easy Servings : 4 Source : Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 cup warm water

1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast

1 tablespoons olive oil

3 tablespoons honey

1 teaspoon salt

3 cups all-purpose flour

Directions

Mix warm water and yeast together in a bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 minutes.

Combine olive oil, honey, and salt in a separate bowl; add 1 cup flour and mix well. Mix yeast mixture into flour mixture. Add remaining flour, 1 cup at a time, until dough is well combined.

Knead dough on a floured work surface until dough is soft, smooth, and elastic, about 8 minutes. Roll dough into 4 calzone shapes.

Notes

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Fill dough with your favorite flavors or if using as a pizza crust, top with your favorite toppings. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.

Dough shoud be as "soft as a horse's nose" after kneading.
