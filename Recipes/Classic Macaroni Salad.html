Classic Macaroni Salad

Side

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 20 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: Serves: 5 (Scaled) Source: Hellmanns.com

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces elbow macaroni (about 1-3/4 cups)
  • 1 1/2 cup mayo
  • 4 Tbsp. vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp. mustard
  • 2 tsp. sugar
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 2 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 2 cup chopped green or red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 large can of sliced olives

Directions

Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain and rinse with cold water until completely cool.

Combine mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, sugar, salt and pepper in large bowl. Stir in macaroni, celery, green pepper, olive and onion. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
