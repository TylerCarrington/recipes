Make Your Own Honey Roasted Barbeque Sauce (Aka Chick Fil a Sauce)

Uncategorized

Difficulty: Easy Source: Kojo-designs.com

Ingredients

  • 1 c mayonaise
  • 1/4 c mustard
  • 3 Tbs honey
  • 1/3 c barbeque sauce
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions

Whisk together until combined
