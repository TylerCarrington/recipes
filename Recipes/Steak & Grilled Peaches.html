Steak & Grilled Peaches

★★★★★

Dinner

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 Source: Better Homes & Gardens

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp lemon-pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 Tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 lbs Steak
  • 6 peaches
  • 2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp snipped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 Tbsp canola oil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Directions

In a small bowl stir together paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, lemon-pepper, cumin, crushed red pepper, salt, and black pepper. Rub onto both sides of steaks.

Grill steaks for 10 to 13 minutes. Cover and let stand 5 minutes.

Grill peaches for 5 minutes turning once.

Meanwhile, combine balsamic vinegar, snipped thyme, oil, sugar, 1/4 tsp black pepper. Add peaches and toss.

Serve with steak; top with thyme sprigs.
