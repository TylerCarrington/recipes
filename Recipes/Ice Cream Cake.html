Ice Cream Cake

★★★

Dessert

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 8 Source: Sortedfood.com

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup chocolate
  • 6 cups rice krispies
  • 4 tbsp Cocoa powder
  • 28 oreos (3/4 package)
  • 6 overripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Directions

OVERNIGHT PREPARATION

Slice the bananas into thin chunks and place onto a baking tray lined with greaseproof paper (they can overlap a bit). Freeze the banana slices overnight.

MAKE THE BASE AND TOP

Crush the oreos in a ziplock bag and melt the oil in a small pan or a microwave. Stir the oil into the biscuits and press the mixture into a loose bottomed springform pan.

MAKE THE RICE KRISPIES TOP

Melt the chocolate and almond butter in the microwave for about 60 seconds. Stir with rice krispies and sea salt in large bowl until well combined.

MAKE THE ICE CREAM

Place the frozen bananas into a blender with the cocoa powder and vanilla extract. Blitz to a very smooth puree. Pour in a little water if it’s not coming together. Spread your banana ice cream over the cooled base, then top with the rice krispie mix. Freeze the whole thing for a few hours.

SERVE

Optional: Melt the chocolate in a microwave and it drizzle over the cake. Freeze for 5 more minutes before serving.
