Condiment

Prep Time: 5 minutes Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 can (2 oz) flat anchovies
  • 1/3 cup Olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp dry mustard
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayo

Directions

Add ingredients to blender and server.
