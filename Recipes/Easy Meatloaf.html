Easy Meatloaf

Dinner

Prep Time : 10 m Cook Time : 1 h Difficulty : Easy Servings : 8 Source : Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1/2 cup applesauce

1 onion, chopped

4 slices of bread

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion, salt, applesauce and bread.

Mix thoroughly by hand

Press into pan

Bake for 1 hour

Notes

Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
