Apple Stuffed Chicken Breast

Chicken, Dinner

Prep Time: 15 m Cook Time: 25 m Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup chopped apple
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 1 tablespoon Italian-style dried bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch

Directions

Combine apple, applesauce, and bread crumbs. Set aside.

Flatten chicken breasts between sheets of waxed paper to 1/4 inch thickness. Divide apple mixture between chicken breasts, and roll up each breast. Secure with toothpicks.

Add olive oil to a 7 inch skillet over medium heat. Brown stuffed chicken breasts. Add wine and 1/4 cup water. Cover. Simmer for 15 to 20 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink.

Transfer chicken to a serving platter. Combine 1 tablespoon water and cornstarch; stir into juices in pan. Cook and stir until thickened. Pour gravy over chicken, and garnish with parsley. Serve.
