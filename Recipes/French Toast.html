French Toast

Uncategorized

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 slices

Ingredients

  • 1.25 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla (if not in almond milk)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 dash cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 8 slices bread

Directions

Lightly toast 8 slices of bread.

Mix milk, flour, vanilla, salt, cinnamon, sugar, and cornstarch. Whisk together until combined.

Dip bread in mixture and cook until golden brown.
