Boxed Chocolate Cupcake

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 20 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 20 cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp ground flax seeds
  • 2.5 Tbsp water
  • 1 box Duncan Hines classic white cake
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions

Mix flax seeds and 2.5 Tbsp water in a small dish to create flax egg. Refrigerate for 5 minutes.

Preheat oven to 350. Line cupcake pan with liners.

Combine cake mix, applesauce, cocoa powder, 1 cup water, vegetable oil, and flax egg. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.

Spoon evenly into cupcake liners and cook for 18 minutes.
