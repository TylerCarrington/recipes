Blueberry Muffins

Breakfast, Dessert

Prep Time : 15 m Cook Time : 25 m Difficulty : Easy Servings : 8 Source : Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

3/4 cup white sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoons baking powder

1/3 cup vegetable oil

1 flax egg (2.5 Tbsp water, 1 Tbsp flax seed)

1/3 cup unsweetened plain almond milk

1 cup fresh blueberries

1.5 Tablespoons poppy seeds (optional)


Topping:

1/2 cup white sugar

1/3 cup all-purpose flour

1/4 cup vegan butter, cubed, softened

1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners.

Combine 1 1/2 cups flour, 3/4 cup sugar, salt and baking powder. Place vegetable oil into a 1 cup measuring cup; add the egg and enough milk to fill the cup. Mix this with flour mixture. Fold in blueberries. Fill muffin cups right to the top, and sprinkle with crumb topping mixture.

To Make Crumb Topping: Mix together 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup flour, 1/4 cup butter, and 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon. Mix with fork, and sprinkle over muffins before baking.

Bake for 25 - 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until done.

Notes

Aluminum foil helps keep food moist, ensures it cooks evenly, keeps leftovers fresh, and makes clean-up easy.
