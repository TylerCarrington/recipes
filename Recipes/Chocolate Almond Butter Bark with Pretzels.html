Chocolate Almond Butter Bark with Pretzels

Dessert

Prep Time: 23 hrs 20 min Cook Time: 5 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 20 Source: Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, very finely chopped
  • Â½ cup Almond butter
  • 2 tablespoons Buttery Spread
  • Pretzels

Directions

Place a double broiler over low heat and slowly melt chocolate, stirring regularly.

Meanwhile, microwave to melt almond butter and butter in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring every 20 seconds until melted. Add more butter if the mixture seems too thick.

Pour melted chocolate onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and spread into an even layer. Dollop lines of melted peanut butter on top of chocolate and run a toothpick through the mixture to form decorative swirls.

Before the chocolate hardens, sprinkle with pretzels, and other toppings, as desired. Place in fridge or freezer until set, and break into pieces to serve.
