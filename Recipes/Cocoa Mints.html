Cocoa Mints

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep Time: 2 hrs Cook Time: 8 min Difficulty: Hard Servings: 36 mint sandwiches Source: Grandma K

Ingredients

  • Mints:
  • 3/4 cup shortening
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 1/4 cup vanilla almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • Mint filling:
  • 3 Tbsp room temperature vegan butter
  • 1.5 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 Tbsp vanilla almond milk
  • 5 drops mint extract

Directions

Combine shortening, sugar and applesauce. In a separate bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add cocoa gradually to shortening and sugar. Add milk and vanilla and mix well. Slowly add dry mixture until well combined. Cool in refrigerator for at least 1 hour or until it handles easily.

Preheat oven to 325. Flour surface and roll out dough to 1/8 inch thick. Cut out circles about the size of a 50 cent peice. Cook for 8 minutes. Let cool completely before icing.

For icing, beat ingredients until well combined.
