Slow Cooker Sloppy Joes

Dinner

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 3 hrs Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 to 6

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 chopped sweet onion
  • 1 medium red bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup bbq sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Apple cider vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Buns

Directions

Brown ground beef, onion, bell pepper, and garlic on medium-high heat. Drain fat.

Transfer beef mixture to pot. Add ketchup, bbq sauce, Worcestershire, brown sugar, and chili powder. Stir to combine.

Cook on high in slow cooker for 3 hours. Serve hot.
