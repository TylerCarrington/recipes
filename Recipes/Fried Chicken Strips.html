Fried Chicken Strips

Dinner

Prep Time : 30 mins Cook Time : 14 mins Difficulty : Medium Servings : Serves: 4 servings Source : Godairyfree.org

Ingredients

Up to 2 lbs Chicken

Vegetable oil


Vegan Buttermilk:

½ cup + 2 tablespoons all purpose flour

2 tablespoons non-GMO cornstarch

1 cup cold unsweetened almond milk beverage

1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar


Breading:

1 cup whole wheat bread crumbs

1 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions

Cut chicken into strips or nuggets. Salt and pepper and set aside.

Fill a skillet with about an inch of vegetable oil and began to heat. You will want the oil to be about 350 degrees when you start frying the chicken.

Get two bowls for batter and breading. In one bowl, dump in the flour and cornstarch. Add about half of the almond milk and stir vigorously with a fork to dissolve. Add the rest of the almond milk and the apple cider vinegar, and stir to incorporate.

In the other bowl, mix together the bread crumbs and salt.

Assemble the chicken strips:

Get a conveyor belt going. From left to right, have the chicken, the vegan buttermilk, the breadcrumbs mixture and lastly the baking sheet. Dip each chicken slice into the the buttermilk, letting the excess drip off. Transfer to the breadcrumbs bowl and use the other hand to sprinkle a handful of breadcrumbs over the chicken, to coat completely.

Place chicken in hot skillet, making sure the chicken falls away from you when setting in pan as to not splash yourself.
Flip chicken with metal tongs when you see it browning on the oil side. Take out once both sides are fried and place onto a plate with a paper towel to remove any excess oil.

Repeat for all strips. Enjoy.
