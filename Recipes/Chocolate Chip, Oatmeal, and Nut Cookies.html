Chocolate Chip, Oatmeal, and Nut Cookies

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep Time: 15 m Cook Time: 10 m Difficulty: Easy Servings: 24 Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup almond milk
  • 1/3 cup almond butter
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (220 degrees C). Oil a large baking sheet.

Stir sugar, almond milk, almond butter, canola oil, and vanilla extract together with a whisk in a large bowl until completely smooth.

Mix flour, oats, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; add to the peanut butter mixture and stir to combine. Fold chocolate chips and walnut pieces into the flour mixture.

Drop your batter by large spoonfuls onto prepared baking sheet.

Bake cookies in preheated oven until browned along the edges, about 10 minutes. Cool cookies on sheet for 10 minutes before removing to a cooling rack to cool completely.
