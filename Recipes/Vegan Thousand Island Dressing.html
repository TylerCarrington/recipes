Vegan Thousand Island Dressing

Condiment

Difficulty: Easy Source: Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C. Earth Balance® MindfulMayo™
  • 1/4 tsp. onion powder
  • 2 Tb. ketchup
  • 2 tb. sweet pickle relish
  • sea salt to taste

Directions

Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl until completely smooth.
