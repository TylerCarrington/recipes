Vegan Brownies

Dessert

Prep Time: 15 m Cook Time: 25 m Difficulty: Easy Servings: 16 Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup applesauce

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt. Pour in water, vegetable oil, vanilla, and applesauce; mix until well blended. Spread evenly in a 9x9 inch baking pan.

Bake for about 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until the top is no longer shiny. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before cutting into squares.

Notes

Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
