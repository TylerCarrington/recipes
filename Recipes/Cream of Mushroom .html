Cream of Mushroom

★★★★★

Side

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 20 min Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp vegan butter or olive oil
  • 1 cup onion, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Sea salt and black pepper
  • 1 cup finely chopped mushrooms
  • 2 Tbsp all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup unsweetened plain almond milk

Directions

In a large pan over medium heat, add vegan butter or olive oil, onions and garlic. Season with salt and pepper and stir. Cook for 2-3 minutes, then add mushrooms. Cook for 3-4 minutes more or until lightly browned.

Sprinkle in flour and whisk to stir and coat the veggies. Cook for 1 minute, then slowly add in veggie stock, soy sauce, and Worcestershire, whisking until incorporated.

Once incorporated, Add almond milk next and whisk to stir again. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low to thicken. Cook for 5-7 minutes more, or until thick and bubbly.
