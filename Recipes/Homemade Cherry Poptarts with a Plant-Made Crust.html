Homemade Cherry Poptarts with a Plant-Made Crust

Breakfast

Difficulty: Easy Source: Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

  • POPTART PASTRY DOUGH:
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons coconut sugar
  • 1 cup cold Earth Balance® Original Buttery Spread
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup Earth Balance® Original Buttery Spread, melted to spread on crust before baking
  • CHERRY FILLING:
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon lukewarm water
  • 2 cups cherries, pitted and stemmed
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • CHERRY ICING:
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cherry filling
  • 1 teaspoon almond milk, plus more to reach desired consistency

Directions

To the bowl of a food processor, add flour, salt and sugar, pulsing until combined. Add butter, pulsing until well incorporated. With processor running, add vanilla and cold water, mixing until just combined. Roll dough into a ball, cover with cling wrap and chill for at least 20 minutes.

While dough is chilling, prepare the cherry filling. In a small bowl, combine cornstarch and water, and set aside.

In a medium saucepan over medium heat, cook the cherries, sugar, lemon juice and salt for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in cornstarch mixture and bring to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Remove to a bowl and allow to cool before pulsing in a food processor.

Preheat oven to 375°F.

On a floured surface, roll out dough and use a pizza cutter to make rectangles in the size you'd like your poptarts.

Arrange half of the dough rectangles on a parchment-lined baking sheet and top with 2 tablespoons of cherry filling. Using a fork, poke holes in the remaining dough rectangles before placing over the cherry filling. Crimp the edges with a fork to seal.

Brush the tops with melted Earth Balance® Original Buttery Spread and bake for 30 minutes, until golden brown.

While poptarts are baking, prepare the cherry icing by combining all ingredients in a small bowl, adding more almond milk to reach desired consistency. Allow poptarts to cool before drizzling with icing.
