Sugar Cookies + Easy Vegan Frosting

Dessert

Difficulty: Easy Source: Earthbalancenatural.com

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups vegan butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 flax egg (2.5 Tbs water + 1 flax seed)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Earth Balance® Organic Buttery Spread, whipped
  • 1 tablespoon soy milk, vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions

Preheat oven to 350F.

In small cup, mix together ground flax seeds and water to make one

In large bowl, mix together butter and sugar until fluffy using electric beaters. Beat in flax egg and vanilla.

Using a large wooden spoon, stir in baking powder and flour, first adding 2 cups, then gradually adding in the last two cups as you continue to stir. The dough will seem a bit dry at first but keep stirring and it will soften up. If you're really struggling, just add a touch more water.

Cover and chill dough for 1 hour.

Once dough is chilled, roll dough out onto floured, flat surface to 1/4

Cut out desired shapes and bake on greased cookie sheet for 8-10 minutes. I like mine a bit softer so I remove them right at the 8-minute mark!

Remove from cookie sheet and allow to cool on wire rack. Be sure to cool completely before frosting. And if you're using sprinkles, add them before you bake the dough!

FOR THE FROSTING: In large bowl, add powdered sugar, then mix in Earth Balance® Buttery Spread by hand with large wooden spoon.

Stir in vanilla. Slowly add soy milk 1 tablespoon at a time.

Continue to stir until there are no lumps. You could make it thicker or thinner by adjusting the powdered sugar/milk ratio but I prefer mine a bit thicker for easier spreading.
